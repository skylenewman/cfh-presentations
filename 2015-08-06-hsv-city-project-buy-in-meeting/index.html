A Code for America Brigade

What is a Brigade?

"Code for America Brigades are volunteer groups that collaborate with local government and community partners to build new tools that help with local civic issues."

- https://www.codeforamerica.org/brigade/about/

The "Four Ps" of Brigades

  1. People power Brigades, and everyone is invited to join!
  2. Participation allows our network to grow and sustain.
  3. Plans enable Brigades to set goals and measure outcomes.
  4. Partnerships with local community organizations and government are at the core of Brigades. Collaborating with partners is key to building civic technology that works for everyone.

- http://www.codeforamerica.org/brigade/organize/

Four Common Activities of Brigades

  1. Leading & Organizing
  2. Opening civic data
  3. Advocating for open government
  4. Deploying & hacking civic apps

Recovers

Recovers.org is a toolkit for community powered disaster recovery. The disaster response platform at [townname].recovers.org incorporates searchable databases, social media aggregation, and mobile tools, allowing grassroots organizers to efficiently marshal resources in their area.

Florence, AL is using this service.
